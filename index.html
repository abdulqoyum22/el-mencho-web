EL MENCHO NARRATIVE

El Mencho ($MENCHO) started as a meme coin built around breaking news and online hype. At the beginning it was mostly about shock value and trending topics. Now the story has changed. The original developer is no longer active so the community took over. This is called a Community Takeover or CTO. Mint and freeze permissions have been removed and liquidity is secured. Because of this the holders are now in control. The focus is no longer on real world events. Instead the goal is to build a strong community project. The plan is steady growth, clear communication, active members and long term progress. What started as chaos is now a community led rebuild. The future of $MENCHO is in the hands of the holders.
